What is the apprenticeship levy?

As of 07 April 2017, if your business has a PAYE of over £3m a year, you’ll have to pay a mandatory levy of 0.5%. The good news is, the government will give you it back as a time limited electronic voucher to buy training from recognised providers like us.

You’ll also receive an allowance of £15,000 to claim back to fund apprenticeship training.

We can help you develop a strategy that efficiently re-invests the entire levy back into your organisation, helping discover some of the brightest new talents emerging in your sector.
